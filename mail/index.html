Android Workshop

About Us

We are a team of IIT Bombay students experienced in Android app development. As you may know, it is being increasingly accepted as a norm of the future, and companies across the world are actively looking to recruit students with expertise in Android app development. Our rendezvous with cutting-edge technology has convinced us that Android app development, while being extremely easy to learn, is a difficult art to master. We are looking to share the skills in Android development we have amassed over the last few years, and this workshop is a humble attempt of ours in this direction. For further details on our core team, please view the presentation attached.

What we offer

We plan to conduct a 20-hour workshop aimed at delivering a holistic understanding of the nuances of Android app development. What makes our workshop unique is that it is completely hands-on. Our experienced workforce will be by the side of the students throughout, helping them so that they may exploit their programming skills to the fullest.

We also intend to assign live projects to students, and the 'Best apps' shall be suitably rewarded. The live projects shall be a valuable takeaway for the students, and will strengthen their resume to no small extent. The certificate that the students receive on successful completion of the workshop will also be a great addition to their profile.
